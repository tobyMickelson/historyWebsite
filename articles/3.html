World War II

Article 3 - Toby

Which battle in the Pacific Theater played the greatest role in the outcome of the war? Why?

The attack on Pearl Harbor was the most important battle in the Pacific because it got the United States into World War 2 and it destroyed Pearl Harbor.

Soon after Japan attacked Pearl Harbor, the United States decided to join the second World War. This majorly affected the outcome of the war because one of the world’s largest superpowers joined this war. The United States joining World War 2 helped cause D-Day.

The second reason that the attack on Pearl Harbor was the most important battle is that this attack destroyed Pearl Harbor. When Japan planned the attack on Pearl Harbor, they planned to keep the United States from joining the second World War.
